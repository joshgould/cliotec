Applications

Clio Technologies Technologies' products are used in a wide variety of applications, such as military, avionics, industrial and computer displays.

High Performance Displays

Clio Technologies provides products used in the high performance lighting displays, including dual mode (day and night vision) operation. Its products include:

High efficiency, dual mode backlight light guides, which are custom made for various configurations and dimensions, including square displays.

Precision cut optical films for easy installation. Optical films include backlight enhancement films, dual brightness enhancement films, diffusers, adhesives, angle expanding films, reflectors, and special films.

RGB LED backlights for saturated color for both day and night vision applications.

Industrial Displays

Clio offers products for simulating displays and outdoor industrial use including:

Backlight guides for simulating displays, which can be used for dual mode (day or night) applications, even under very bright sunlight.

LED drivers for both digital and analog dimming control. The drivers can protect against open circuit, under voltage, soft start, and over temperature conditions.

LED strips and CCFL for high brightness and energy efficient LCD backlights. All of the products can be custom made, to meet your special requirements.
