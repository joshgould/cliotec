About Clio Technologies

Clio Technologies designs and produces unique light guide architectures and backlighting solutions. Our designs offer superior brightness, dimming capabilities, and high efficiency for clients throughout North America and Europe.

Clio Technologies designs and produces solutions for the military, aerospace, and industrial markets. Our backlighting solutions have consistently met and exceeded our clients' most stringent expectations and requirements for land, air, sea, and space applications and for applications that require day and night vision capabilities.

Since 1994, Clio Technologies has developed custom LCD backlighting solutions for a broad range of applications for the military, aerospace, and industrial markets, including commercial and military avionics, ground vehicles, space infrared applications, and satellites.

Our Products

Divergent Angle Rotator backlight light guide (light pipe with microprisms on both surfaces). This technology is particularly suitable for dual mode applications. With a single light guide, we can achieve uniform and efficient illumination for both day and night vision modes.

High Temperature Light Guides, which are made of high temperature materials for military and avionics applications.

Backlight systems for LCDs, including Collimator LCD modules, which are backlight systems installed in an LCD module.

Custom backlight solutions which meet your stringent dimension, configuration, view angle, dimming, and other specifications.

Backlighting solutions, using either CCFLs or LEDs, as chosen by the client.

Backlight Components, including high quality CCFL lamps, inverters, LEDs, LED driver boards, and precision cut optical and other films.
